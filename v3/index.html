Hello! My name is Low. I am a software engineer based in Malaysia.

I specialize in front-end web development. I started to self-learn web development in February 2020. Before this most of my programming experience has been in Java (basic introductory level) and Python (basic machine learning). I learned how to build web applications through Codecademy, freeCodeCamp, and watching tutorials on Youtube.

I went to school for software engineering, but was not satisfied with what I learned in class. To overcome this, I decided to supplement my formal education with some self-studies. I am currently following the curriculum listed on teachyourselfcs.com. I have just finished the nand2tetris computer architecture course (listed above under Projects), and am now going through the classic programming textbook SICP.

My favorite computer science subdiscipline is deep learning. I wrote an essay on neural networks and how they operate under the hood. Do check it out!

In terms of non-CS activities, I am usually playing the piano, reading a book, or watching a movie/TV series on Netflix.
