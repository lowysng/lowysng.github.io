Nous

Thank you for your interest in Nous! Nous is a learning community consisting of primarily A-level students. Our aim is to provide a platform for students to discuss and ask questions about any problems they might encounter in their studies.

  • Currently stuck in a problem? Show us what you are having trouble with! Our tutor will be more than happy to assist you.
  • Having trouble with a certain topic? Arrange a meeting with one of our tutors (at an available time slot) for a one-on-one session.

To get started, click here to join our Discord server.

We are a newly formed organization, so things may be bumpy for now. Currently, we only support one subject: A-level Mathematics. We are looking to add more subjects in the future, and also expand our services. Please feel free to join our discord community to keep yourself up to date on all currently available subjects, along with future subjects/services. Happy learning!
